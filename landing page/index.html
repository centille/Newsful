Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Newsful? How does it work?

    Newsful is a browser plugin to detect fake news. It traces and tracks fake news and its origin using official sources as the input filter.

  • Does Newsful provide multimedia support?

    Yes, Newsful does provide multimedia support. It enables analysis of text and images to uncover manipulated content, verify sources, and enhance the accuracy of identifying fake news.

  • Does Newsful support multiple languages?

    Yes, Newsful provides multilingual support to ensure the comprehensive analysis of misinformation across all Indian languages, expanding the effectiveness of the detection process.

  • Will Newsful provide credibility checks?

    Yes, Newsful provides credibility checks to assess the reliability of sources, ensuring accurate information and helps to filter out misinformation.

  • Can the plugin detect phishing websites?

    Yes, Newsful detects phishing websites which prevents the spread of malicious links and scams often propagated through deceptive news stories.
